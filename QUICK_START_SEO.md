## üöÄ Next Steps (In Order)

### Step 1: Set Up Google Business Profile (30 minutes)
**Why**: This is the #1 way to appear first in local searches

1. Go to [https://www.google.com/business/](https://www.google.com/business/)
2. Click "Manage now"
3. Enter business information:
   - Name: Greenov Africa Ltd
   - Category: Agricultural Service
   - Location: Kigali, Rwanda
   - Website: https://www.greenovafrica.com/
4. Verify your business (via postcard, phone, or email)
5. Complete your profile:
   - Add business description (see GOOGLE_MY_BUSINESS_SETUP.md)
   - Upload logo as primary photo
   - Add photos of products/services
   - Set business hours
6. **Important**: Set your logo as the PRIMARY PHOTO in Google Business Profile

**See detailed guide**: `GOOGLE_MY_BUSINESS_SETUP.md`

### Step 2: Set Up Google Search Console (15 minutes)
**Why**: This tells Google about your website and helps with indexing

1. Go to [https://search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Add property: `https://www.greenovafrica.com`
3. Verify ownership (DNS method recommended)
4. Submit sitemap: `https://www.greenovafrica.com/sitemap.xml`
5. Request indexing for your homepage

**See detailed guide**: `SEO_OPTIMIZATION_GUIDE.md`

### Step 3: Fix Logo in Search Results (5 minutes)
**Why**: Your logo should appear next to your search result

1. In Google Business Profile, set logo as PRIMARY PHOTO
2. In Google Search Console, use "URL Inspection" tool
3. Enter: `https://www.greenovafrica.com`
4. Click "Request Indexing"
5. Wait 1-2 weeks for Google to update

**Note**: The logo issue is usually because:
- Logo isn't set as primary photo in Google Business Profile
- Google hasn't re-indexed your site yet
- The Open Graph tags are already correct in your HTML ‚úÖ

### Step 4: Get Reviews (Ongoing)
**Why**: Reviews improve your search ranking and visibility

1. Ask satisfied customers to leave Google reviews
2. Respond to ALL reviews (positive and negative)
3. Aim for 10-20 reviews minimum
4. Respond within 24-48 hours

### Step 5: Post Regularly (Weekly)
**Why**: Active profiles rank higher

1. Post updates on Google Business Profile weekly
2. Share:
   - New products/services
   - Company news
   - Event participation
   - Success stories
