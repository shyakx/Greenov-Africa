# Google My Business Setup Guide for Greenov Africa

## Overview
Google My Business (now called Google Business Profile) helps your business appear in Google Search and Maps. This guide will help you set up and optimize your profile to appear first in search results.

## Step 1: Create or Claim Your Business Profile

### 1.1 Go to Google Business Profile
1. Visit [https://www.google.com/business/](https://www.google.com/business/)
2. Click **"Manage now"** or **"Get started"**
3. Sign in with your Google account (use a business email if possible)

### 1.2 Enter Business Information
- **Business name**: Greenov Africa Ltd (or Greenov Africa)
- **Business category**: 
  - Primary: "Agricultural Service"
  - Secondary: "Solar Energy Equipment Supplier", "Agricultural Equipment Supplier"
- **Location**: 
  - If you have a physical location: Enter your address in Kigali, Rwanda
  - If you're service-area only: Select "I deliver goods and services to my customers" and add service areas

### 1.3 Contact Information
- **Phone number**: Your business phone number
- **Website**: https://www.greenovafrica.com/
- **Email**: Your business email (e.g., greenovafrica@gmail.com)

## Step 2: Verify Your Business

### Verification Methods:
1. **Postcard** (Most Common)
   - Google will mail a postcard to your business address
   - Takes 5-7 business days
   - Enter the verification code when received

2. **Phone Verification**
   - Available for some businesses
   - Google will call with a verification code

3. **Email Verification**
   - Available for some businesses
   - Check your business email for verification code

4. **Video Verification**
   - For some businesses, Google may request a video call

**Important**: Complete verification before proceeding. Unverified businesses won't appear in search results.

## Step 3: Complete Your Business Profile

### 3.1 Add Business Description
**Recommended Description:**
```
Greenov Africa Ltd empowers African smallholder farmers with innovative solar-powered cooling technology. We reduce post-harvest losses by up to 60% and improve food security through clean energy solutions. Our off-grid cooling systems require no grid electricity, have zero fuel costs, and produce zero emissions. We provide comprehensive training programs to help farmers maximize the benefits of our technology and improve post-harvest management.

Based in Kigali, Rwanda, we serve farming communities across Africa, providing climate-smart and affordable solutions to tackle the continent's biggest agricultural challenges.
```

**Keywords to include:**
- Solar-powered cooling
- Post-harvest losses
- African farmers
- Clean energy
- Off-grid solutions
- Food security
- Agricultural technology

### 3.2 Add Business Hours
- Set your operating hours (Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 5 PM, or your actual hours)
- Mark special hours for holidays if applicable

### 3.3 Add Photos
**Essential Photos to Add:**
1. **Logo** (Primary photo): Use your logo.jpeg
   - Minimum: 720x720 pixels
   - Recommended: 1200x1200 pixels
   - Format: JPG or PNG

2. **Cover Photo**: A high-quality image of your products/services
   - Recommended: 1024x576 pixels

3. **Product Photos**: Images of your solar cooling systems
4. **Team Photos**: Photos from events (like Rwanda Agrishow, Festival of Cooling)
5. **Office/Workspace Photos**: If applicable

**Photo Tips:**
- Use high-resolution images
- Ensure good lighting
- Show your products/services clearly
- Add photos regularly (Google favors active profiles)

### 3.4 Add Attributes
Select relevant attributes:
- ✅ Women-led business (if applicable)
- ✅ Black-owned business (if applicable)
- ✅ Sustainable practices
- ✅ Offers delivery/service area
- ✅ Online appointments/consultations (if applicable)

## Troubleshooting

### Business Not Appearing in Search
1. **Wait for verification**: Unverified businesses won't appear
2. **Complete profile**: Fill out all sections
3. **Add photos**: Profiles with photos rank better
4. **Get reviews**: Businesses with reviews appear more often
5. **Be patient**: It can take 2-4 weeks after verification

### Logo Not Showing in Search Results
1. **Ensure logo is set as primary photo** in Google Business Profile
2. **Use high-quality image** (at least 720x720 pixels)
3. **Wait for Google to index** (can take 1-2 weeks)
4. **Ensure website has proper Open Graph tags** (already added to index.html)

### Improving Search Rankings
1. **Get more reviews** (aim for 20+)
2. **Post regularly** (at least weekly)
3. **Respond to all reviews**
4. **Keep information updated**
5. **Use relevant keywords** in posts and description
6. **Get local citations** (listings on other directories)

## Important Notes

1. **Consistency**: Use the same business name, address, and phone number everywhere
2. **NAP** (Name, Address, Phone): Must match across all platforms
3. **Regular Activity**: Active profiles rank higher than inactive ones
4. **Reviews**: Quality and quantity both matter
5. **Photos**: Regular photo uploads signal an active business
